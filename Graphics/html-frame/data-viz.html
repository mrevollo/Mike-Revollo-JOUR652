Concussions suffered in high school sports (2005-2006)

Football significantly leads the number of concussions suffered by high school athletes at 201. Girls' soccer comes second in high school athletes who suffer concussions at 51.

Source: Data collected by Journal of Athletic Training

Causes for Concussions (Boys' Soccer)

Within boys' soccer, heading the ball accounts for a little more than 40% of causes for concussions. Attempted slide tackles and blocking shots accounted for concussions at 20.5% and 10.6%, respectively.

Source: Data collected by Journal of Athletic Training
